Our Story

Built on Honesty. Driven by Craftsmanship.

Barnes Garage Doors LLC is a family-owned business built from a simple belief: every customer deserves honest advice, quality work, and a door they're proud of.

Hi, I'm Tanner Barnes.

I started Barnes Garage Doors LLC because I saw too many homeowners getting overcharged for simple repairs or sold doors they didn't need. I believe in doing things differently.

Every job I take on, I approach the way I would if it were my own home — with care, transparency, and a commitment to quality that doesn't cut corners. If something doesn't need replacing, I'll tell you. If there's a better solution, we'll find it together.

We're proud to be an authorized Clopay® dealer, carrying the widest selection of garage doors in North America. That means you get access to premium products paired with a local expert who stands behind the work.
