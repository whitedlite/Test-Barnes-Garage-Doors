Service & Repair

Fast, Honest Repairs — Done Right the First Time.

From broken springs to stuck openers, Tanner and the Barnes team respond quickly and fix it properly. Same-day service available throughout Greater Birmingham.

What We Fix & Service

No job is too big or too small. Here's a look at our most common service calls — if you don't see it, just call us.

🔩

Spring Replacement

Broken torsion or extension springs are a safety hazard. We replace them with high-cycle springs for longer lasting performance — safely and quickly.

⚡

Opener Repair & Replacement

Whether your opener is dead, noisy, or just won't respond, we diagnose and repair or replace it. We install LiftMaster, Chamberlain, and more.

🔗

Cable Repair

Frayed or snapped cables can cause the door to fall dangerously. We replace cables and restore proper balance and tension to your door system.

🛤️

Track Alignment & Repair

Bent, misaligned, or damaged tracks cause binding and noise. We straighten, realign, and replace tracks to get your door rolling smoothly again.

🎰

Roller & Hinge Replacement

Worn rollers and cracked hinges create friction and noise. Replacing them with heavy-duty components makes a dramatic difference in door performance.

📱

Remotes & Keypads

We program and replace remotes, keypads, and smart home integrations. If your existing equipment isn't syncing, we'll get it working or upgrade it.

🪟

Panel Replacement

Storm damage, dents, or cracks in individual panels don't always require a full replacement. We source matching panels and swap them flawlessly.

🌧️

Weatherstripping & Seals

Water intrusion, drafts, and dirt getting in are signs worn weatherstripping needs replacing. We seal your door fully on all four sides.

🔧

Tune-Up & Lubrication

A full tune-up extends door life significantly. We inspect, tighten, lubricate, and adjust your entire door system — and show you how to maintain it.
